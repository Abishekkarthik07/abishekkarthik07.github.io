## MultiAgent-Stock-Advisor

A multi-agent financial portfolio manager combining LLM reasoning with quantitative investing models. The system runs multiple specialized agents (Buffett, Munger, Lynch, etc.) alongside analytical agents for fundamentals, valuation, technicals, and sentiment, and synthesizes their outputs into portfolio actions.

### System Highlights
- Multi-agent reasoning architecture
- Simulation engine and portfolio logs
- Risk management and decision synthesis
