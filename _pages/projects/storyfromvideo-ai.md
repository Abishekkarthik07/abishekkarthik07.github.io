## StoryFromVideo-AI (Vid2Story Generator)

An offline tool to convert short videos into coherent AI-generated stories using Whisper, BLIP, OpenCV and local generative models. Designed for privacy-first, local-only workflows with genre selection and downloadable output.

### Tech Stack
Streamlit, Whisper, BLIP, OpenCV, open-source LLMs
